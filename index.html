About Me

Hello, my name is Cristhal Fuertes. I am a self-motivated Entry-level Full Stack Web Developer with knowledge and proficiency in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and mobile responsive web development, as well as strong skills and ability in writing clean and efficient code.

Recently earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from the Rutgers Division of Continuing Studies, with enhanced skills in problem solving skills and strong ability to execute user friendly applications. Looking to use my certificate in conjunction with my Rutgers degree in Communication in order to utilize my skills to make for the utmost impactful user experience.

With each project, my aim is to engage my audience with an effective, smooth and problem free user-experience. Recently I applied my creative skills in a project where I worked with my team to develop a journal app focused on mental health. I assisted in making for a tranquil app usage with smooth transitions between features as well as creating an aesthetic and calming visual experience. Excited to further enhance my skills in the future as part of a fast-paced, quality-driven team in order to build better experiences for users on the web.
